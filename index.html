Why this app exists

Problem

Travel planning is scattered, generic, and easy to overpack.

Users jump between booking apps, maps, blogs, and chatbots. They still have to repeat personal preferences, check budget fit, avoid disliked experiences, and manually fix unrealistic routing.

Solution

A memory-aware planner that explains every recommendation.

The app converts destination, pickup area, dates, budget, vibe, preferences, dislikes, diet, pace, and sustainability priority into a paced itinerary with ride redirects, JSON export, and judge-readable reasoning.
